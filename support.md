# Next Shot — Support

Next Shot is a GLP-1 medication tracking app for iPhone, made by Dodo Media LLC. This page is here to help you get the most out of the app and to reach us if you need a hand.

## Contact us

Email **[matt@tearaway.app](mailto:matt@tearaway.app)** and we'll get back to you as soon as we can (usually within a couple of business days). Please include your device model and iOS version if you're reporting a problem — it helps us track things down faster.

You can also send feedback right inside the app: open the **Summary** tab and tap **Send feedback**.

## Common questions

### Managing or canceling your subscription
Next Shot Pro is billed through your Apple ID, so subscriptions are managed by Apple — not inside the app:

1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone.
2. Tap your name at the top, then **Subscriptions**.
3. Select **Next Shot** to change your plan, or tap **Cancel Subscription**.

If you cancel, you keep access until the end of the period you already paid for. Canceling a free trial before it ends means you won't be charged.

### Restoring a purchase
If you've subscribed before (for example on another device), open the paywall and tap **Restore Purchases** to bring your subscription back.

### Your data and privacy
Your health data stays **on your device and in your own private iCloud account**. We don't run servers, there's no account to create, and we can't see your health information. Full details are in our [Privacy Policy](https://commissioncrew.github.io/next-shot-pages/).

### Deleting your data
Your records live on your device and your private iCloud. To remove them, delete the Next Shot app; to also clear the synced copy, remove Next Shot's data from **Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage**. The app's **Privacy** settings screen also shows these steps.

## A note on what Next Shot is

Next Shot is a personal **record-keeping tool**. It organizes what you enter — it does **not** provide medical advice and never tells you what dose to take or when. Always follow the guidance of your prescriber and pharmacist.
